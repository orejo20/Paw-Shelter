ABOUT PAW SHELTER

Greetings from PawShelter, where we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of stray dogs and cats. Our goal is to help, care for, and shelter homeless animals while simultaneously speaking out for their welfare and encouraging ethical pet ownership in our neighborhood.


Every animal deserves love, kindness, and a secure place to call home, and that is our belief at Pawshelter. We put up a lot of effort to bring stray dogs and cats off the streets and give them access to warm housing, nourishing food, and medical care. We work hard to rehabilitate these animals and place them in devoted permanent homes with the help of our committed staff and volunteer team.
