What should I consider before adopting a stray animal?

Adopting a stray animal is a wonderful and compassionate decision that can bring immense joy and fulfillment to both you and the animal. Familiarize yourself with the adoption process and requirements of the shelter or rescue organization from which you plan to adopt. Be prepared to complete an application, undergo a screening process, and possibly meet with an adoption counselor to ensure a good match.
